About Us

InAmigos Foundation was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, Founder & CEO. It is a Section 8 non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government and headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. The foundation runs six programs across roughly 28 states, covering food security, education, environmental conservation, women's empowerment, animal welfare and community development.
